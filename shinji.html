SHINJIBOY POGI

To My Son, I hope you know how much I love you so. I might embarrass you from time to time By giving lots of love and needless kisses, But you need to know Mom loves you so. Before you were born I didn't know Just how much I would love you so, But when I looked into your eyes for the first time, I felt my heart grow and grow. A Mother's love is hard to explain, Not everyone will feel the same. Now that you are getting older I felt the need to tell you Just how much I love you so. Time may pass day after day or year after year, But the love I have for you grows nearer and dearer to my heart each day and year. To my son, I hope you know just how much I love you so.
